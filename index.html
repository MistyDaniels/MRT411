Misty Daniels

Caŋté Waśaka Wíŋ

Brave Hearted Woman

Misty Daniels is a Native American student, artist, gamer and creator.

Her passion is the pursuit of growth and rediscovery in regards to her Lakota roots.

She believes that the path to her peoples recovery requires the union of traditional and contemporary methods.

Education

Overview
Misty has spent most of her life on the Fort Peck Reservation in the far north-east corner of Montana. She recieved her AA in Computer Technology from the Fort Peck Tribes Community College in 2015.
She would go on to enroll in the University of Montana in 2018 and recieved her BA in Media Arts in 2020.
Why Work With Misty
Misty's tradition background and modern education offers a unique perspective to todays design. Her will to bring everything she can to the table and desire to exceed expectations through learned skill and passion for growth brings opportunity to those she works with.
